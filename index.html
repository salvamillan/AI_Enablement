Speed support

Helpful steps for broadband speed issues and connection improvement.

1

Run a speed test

Check performance from a device close to the modem to separate WiFi issues from line issues.

2

Restart the modem

A full restart can clear temporary faults and restore connectivity.

3

Check placement

Move the modem away from thick walls and interference for better coverage.
