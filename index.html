Speed support

Helpful steps for broadband speed issues, including a simple reference flow for performance checks.

1

Run a speed test

Check the connection from a device close to the router to separate WiFi issues from line issues.

2

Restart the modem

Power cycling the modem can clear temporary faults and restore normal performance.

3

Check placement

Move the modem away from thick walls and interference to improve coverage and throughput.

Frequently asked questions

Short support-style answers that make the page feel closer to the real eir help flow.

What do the lights mean?

Check the modem lights to see whether power, line and WiFi are all working as expected.

How do I reset WiFi settings?

Use the modem reset procedure to restore the default network name and password.

Why is my broadband slow?

Test near the modem, restart it, and check for coverage or interference issues.
